About

Find Out More About Us

Established in the year 1998 as ‘Central India Civil Erectors’ (CICE) is now a well-known company re-constituted as ‘Mid India Civil Erectors Pvt. Ltd’ (‘MICEPL’) providing quality construction within stipulated time frames, thus, gaining a competitive edge in the construction industry all over. The company is now registered in class ‘A’ (Highest Class of Contractors) with Madhya Pradesh Public Works Department (M.P. P.W.D.) Bhopal and is thus eligible to undertake works of unlimited cost.

  • Godowns and Halls– Storages of various types of goods and exhibition Halls

  • Roads – Rural Roads, State Highways, National and Express Highways
